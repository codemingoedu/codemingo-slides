What is Codemingo?

Codemingo is where tech learners become tech builders. A vibrant community for developers, designers, and creators to learn, build, grow, and launch together.

Whether you're just starting or already building, Codemingo is where collaboration meets progress.

Onboarding Process

1

Join Discord

Connect with our community server and introduce yourself in the #welcome channel.

2

Choose Your Track

Select your area of interest: Frontend, Backend, UI/UX Design, or Open Source.

3

Complete a Starter Challenge

Build a small project to demonstrate your skills and commitment.

4

Be Active and Helpful

Engage with the community, ask questions, and help others when you can.

5

Earn Builder Role

Unlock access to collaborative projects and advanced resources.
