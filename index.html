All project updates, pull requests, and issues are automatically shared in the #github-feeds channel, keeping everyone informed about project progress.

How to Join the Build Crew

Unlock Project Access

To access project channels, complete your first starter challenge. Post your work in #submit-task, and once approved, you'll get the Builder Role.

The Builder Role gives you access to all project channels, collaboration opportunities, and advanced resources.
